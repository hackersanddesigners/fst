This filter refers to the publisher and/or producing entity of a publication.* Through this filter, it is possible to identify, whether there is any monopole in terms of the publishers of global scholarly publishing. In the pie charts, it becomes visible that Springer, Cambridge University Press, and Oxford University Press are the publishers most frequently named as producing entity. This suggests that current scholarly publishing is centred in the Western hemisphere.



*This filter is an interpretation of 260 - Publication, Distribution, etc. (Imprint) (R), subfield code $b - Name of publisher, distributor, etc. (R), as well as tag 264 - Production, Publication, Distribution, Manufacture, and Copyright Notice (R), subfield code $b - Name of producer, publisher, distributor, manufacturer (R). According to staff members of the Utrecht University library, either tag 260 or tag 264 are filled in for an item.
