For the data set of the Feminist Search Tool, Read-in made a selection of the records of the Utrecht University library on the basis of the dates of publication. Therefore, the time period of the publications of this data set are works published between 2006 and 2016. Read-in selected this specific time-frame in order to attain a data set that would be manageable to work with in the limited time we were given.
